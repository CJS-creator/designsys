# DesignForge Project Roadmap

## Vision

DesignForge aims to become the leading design system management platform, enabling teams to create, maintain, and scale their design systems with ease. Our vision is to democratize design system creation and make it accessible to teams of all sizes.

---

## Strategic Goals

| Goal | Timeline | Success Metric |
|------|----------|----------------|
| Establish DesignForge as industry standard | 2 years | 10,000+ active users |
| Launch enterprise features | Q3 2024 | 50+ enterprise customers |
| Achieve 99.9% uptime | Q4 2024 | SLA compliance |
| Expand to mobile platforms | Q2 2025 | iOS/Android SDKs |
